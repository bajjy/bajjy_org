## What we make

A small lab that designs and ships **web-native systems** — products, tools, and
experiments. The bias is toward simple infrastructure, framework-light frontends,
and code you can read in one sitting.

## How we work

A typical engagement runs as a **tight loop with the founder or product lead**.
We start from the core mechanic of the product — the thing that has to feel right
— and grow outward from there. No long discovery phases.

* Greenfield builds where the studio owns the stack
* Rescue work on Node / Vue / vanilla codebases
* Game-tech R&D — tactics, real-time, dossier-style interfaces
* Internal automation that replaces three SaaS subscriptions with one tiny service

## What we don't do

Endless re-platforming. Marketing sites that aren't load-bearing. Anything where
"AI strategy" is the actual brief.
